About Us


City's pressing food waste challenge. With approximately
9.5 million tons of food waste generated annually in the UK, and a significant portion originating from the hospitality and food service sector, there is an urgent need for action.

CollectForLives operates by forming direct partnerships with food franchises to intercept surplus food and redistribute it to local communities in need. Our intuitive mobile application empowers volunteers to play a crucial role in this process, fostering community engagement and collaboration.

By leveraging technology and grassroots efforts, CollectForLives not only reduces food waste but also combats food insecurity, striving to create a more sustainable and compassionate city. Explore our website to learn more about our mission, initiatives, and how you can get involved in making a positive impact.
